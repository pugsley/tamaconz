Starboard
Chief Technology Officer
2019 — 2025

Starboard is world-leading maritime domain awareness software. It helps governments and organisations combat illegal fishing, prevent transnational crime, and protect offshore assets.

It’s a complex, high-performing geospatial platform that elegantly integrates 30+ data sources and ~7,000 streaming data points per second, providing a real-time view of activity across the world’s oceans.

As Chief Technology Officer, I played a key role in product development, creating the engineering team and culture, and ensuring the Starboard platform remained secure and reliable. We had an incredible team with a close-knit, high-performing culture, consistently shipping software our customers love and rely on daily.

Winners at the 2024 New Zealand High Tech Awards for:

  • Most Innovative Hi-Tech Software Solution
  • Hi-Tech Startup Company of the Year

Goodworld
Chief Technology Officer
2016 — 2019

Goodworld is an innovative fundraising platform for non-profits, originally launched as the world’s first technology enabling donations via hashtags on social media.

It’s a sophisticated system that integrates APIs from social media platforms, payment gateways, and other services, enabling non-profits to seamlessly raise funds from their supporters.

As a founding team member, I led the engineering team and built the product from the ground up, shaping its development from inception.
